Hackathon Rules
---------------

*   Participants must be 18 years or older.
*   Submissions must include a video with a demo, a proper README file and applicable documentation.
*   Each participant may submit only one Project for each Track.
*   All winners must complete ID verification to receive prizes.
*   Submissions must be completed individually. Team submissions will not be accepted.
*   All code submitted by the participants must be open source.
*   All project submissions must have a MIT License
*   Employees and contractors of Kraken may participate but are not eligible to receive prizes
*   Participants who fail to comply with these Rules may be disqualified at any time, and any prizes awarded may be revoked
